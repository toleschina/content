Directory

Register

This Directory is open to all former pupils, parents and grandparents to register online. Former parents and grandparents have a huge involvement with the School while their families are at the School and we are keen to continue this relationship beyond the end of their School career. If you would like to join the online Strathallian Community, please click the register button below.

Search Directory

You can search for a former pupil, by either Name, House, Home Location or any combination of the three by using the boxes above. Only one field needs to be completed to carry out a search, but the more detail you enter the fewer and more specific the results returned.

You will notice that some fields will be seen on the website, some will be hidden and viewable only by you and the School, and the third section you can choose to show or hide. The Strathallian Club uses information entered here to update the database of contact details.

Please note that not every former pupil is listed but only those who have chosen to enter their details in the directory. Neither the School nor the Strathallian Club will enter details to be viewed on the internet.
