Cycling

Youth Tour of Scotland – Road Cycling

Mark McGuire took part in the 3 day Youth Tour of Scotland Event held here at Strathallan over the Easter holidays. There were 4 stages to the event and after crashing on stage 1 Mark got back on his bike and caught the leading bunch after an epic chase. In Stage 3 he was looking good for a top finish when he got a puncture. He finished 11th overall and was the 2nd best Scottish Rider.

On his Mountain Bike, Mark has had 2nd place in 2 Scottish races and a 6th finish in a British Race so far this season.
