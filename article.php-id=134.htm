Back to Latest News

Skiing

National Selection

Chris Guest, Kirsty Guest, Fraser Buchan and Katie Guest have been selected for the Scottish Alpine Ski Team. Katie has also received a Bank of Scotland Sports Aid Award to assist with her skiing.

Girls Hockey

Midland District

The following have been selected for the District Hockey teams.

Midland U14: Imogen Robertson Barnett, Caitlin Donald and Polly Lightbody

They compete at the inter district tournament this Sunday at Peffermill.

Midland U15: Rosalind Coutts, Raina Dalrymple, Abbie Lindsay, Zoe Morris, Kelly MacKenzie, Pippa Wood, Isla Gordon

These girls have been selected in the training squad preparing for the tournament in June.

Natonal Selection

Megan Inch has been named in the Scotland U18 team to compete in the European Championships in Catania in July. This is a particular achievement because Megan is still eligible to play in the National U16 age group.

Boys Hockey

Motorsport

Nigeria Racing Eagle Driver Unveiling Event

Ovie Iroro has been officially named as a driver with Nigeria Racing Eagle, an Audi-powered team featuring drivers from Africa. The drivers were unveiled in London on the 8th February 2013 after the launch of the British GT Formula 3 Series, where the team is registered to race.

Canoe Slalom

GB Selection

Following the Canoe Slalom 2013 Selection Series held at Holme Pierrepont and Lee Valley Whitewater Centre in April, Eilidh Gibson has been selected as a reserve for the GB Senior Team for the World Cup Race 5 in Bratislava, Slovakia in August. She has also qualified for the selection races in 2014 which is a tremendous achievement as only those paddlers who perform at a high standard are actually allowed to go the selection races for the Senior and U23 teams.

Chess

Megafinals, Delancey UK Schools Chess Challenge, Edinburgh

Seven pupils took part in the Chess Megafinals of the Delancey UK Schools Challenge in Edinburgh last Sunday. Over 120 Competitors were taking part 

Hermes and Orpheas Manos performed well winning 3 out of 6 games and narrowly missing qualification for the next round.

Harrison Tsui won 4 matches, and Derek Lai won 5, so Harrison and Derek have both qualified for the "Gigafinals" (semi-finals) which will take place in July in the North of England.
