Woodlands House

What defines Woodlands House?

There are many potential answers to this, one could be the unstinting encouragement the Tods instilled in us all to create a bond which delivers a sisterhood, a home, a determination to achieve and the ability to recognise each other’s frailties.

A few years ago our outgoing Head of House wrote this in the Strathallian magazine to sum up her experience of Woodlands over the years.  Woodlands essentially is a happy and supportive home from home.  The “ethos” is for the girls to strive to make the most of opportunities and to give of their best in whatever they undertake whether in their studies, sports, music or simply in the respect they show for one another.  There is an emphasis on community, cooperation and upholding the reputation of the House within the School.  We encourage the girls to participate in all aspects of school life and to this end we enjoy both the involvement and, often, victory in inter house competitions!

HOUSEMASTER AND MISTRESS - Mr & Mrs Morc Tod

Woodlands is run by Morc and Abi Tod.  Morc teaches Art and coaches rugby and athletics.  Abi used to teach English but is currently “Mum” to their 3 children – Calum, Fionn and Zac and coaches netball.
