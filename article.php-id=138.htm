News Update - 7th June 2013

Back to Latest News

Mathematics Challenges

In the UK Junior Maths competitions pupils were awarded six gold, fourteen silver and thirteen bronze.

Gold awards went to Caitlin Donald, Andrew Smith, Lyle Ross, Seamus Blanche, Douglas Tait and Ross Campbell.

Best in Year was awarded to Caitlin Donald and Andrew Smith, with Best in School to Andrew Smith who will take part in this year’s Olympiad next week.

Vladimir Ivanov, Caitlin Donald, James Day and Andrew Smith all took part in the regional final of the UK Team Maths Challenge in Edinburgh.

Scottish Maths Challenge

In the Junior competition Andrew Smith gained a gold certificate and James Day silver.

In the Intermediate competition Tony Tong and Finlay Stewart both gained gold certificates.

Equestrian

Pony Club

Georgia Laird won the 1.05 metre Open class at the Fife Hunt Pony Club Summer Show over half term.

Vaulting

Atholl Pettinger finished 1st in the individual and team Equestrian Vaulting Championships in Wales at the weekend. He also received a trophy for finishing 1st in Great Britain in 2012.

Swimming

Midland District Distance Meet, Dundee

Jordan Curtis, Ryan Curtis and Sarah Cameron-Sutcliffe all swam at the Midlands District Distance Meet over the half term weekend.

Jordan swam 4 races and recorded 4 PB’s 3 x Bronze 400 Free, 800 Free, 1500 Free
4th Place 200 free
Ryan swam 4 events and recorded 4 PB’s Bronze 800 Free
Sarah swam 4 events and recorded 3 PB’s    

Alloa Graded Meet, Stirling

Lauren Dundee had a great weekend in Stirling competing in 5 events and recording 5 PB’s in the Alloa Graded Meet.

Boys Hockey

National U16 Selection

Rhys Connah and Cameron Griffiths have been selected for the Scotland U16 team to play against Ulster at the end of June. The matches will take place at Inverclyde, Largs.

Jamie Miller and Iain Robertson have been invited to attend the National U16 Training Camp down in July.

District Tournament

Our U15 players played for Midland District at the inter district tournament on Sunday. The girls team finished in 3rd place and was captained by Raina Dalrymple while the boys won the tournament and were captained by Adam Smith. Adam, Cameron Griffiths, James Day, Iain Robertson, Jamie Miller and Ewan Doig received Gold medals.

Mountain Biking

UK National Series MTB Event

Mark McGuire raced in Shropshire last weekend and came 2nd in the UK National Series MTB Event. This was a great achievement and puts Mark further up the ranking order in the UK. After 3 races he is certainly in the top 3 and may even be at No 1. This is all looking good for Mark’s target of making the Scottish team for European Championships in Austria in August.
